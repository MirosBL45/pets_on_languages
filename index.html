pets - medicine for the soul

how to choose a pet

Regardless of all the complications that pets bring, the amount of love and happiness that animals bring to a home is immeasurable and worth every effort.

The best pets - cats and dogs

The best pets are cats and dogs. At least that's what the number of families who narrowed their selection down to these two categories says. The choice can be difficult, because many factors separate them into two completely different categories.

but how to choose?

Dogs are the ones that require much more time, attention and money. They are generally considered to be much more social than cats. On the other hand, cats are quite independent, they are much easier to care for, they are quite simple and take up a smaller part of the household budget.

you need to think carefully

A dog is a very intelligent animal with which you will not have many problems. You can teach him some neat tricks. The independence of a cat as a pet can also be an advantage, because you won't have to worry when you leave the apartment for work and obligations.

think about yourself

Cats are really good pets that you can cuddle a lot with, and if you don't have plenty of time it can be a good choice. On the other hand, if you have more time to devote to a pet, consider a dog because you can take it for long walks, runs and even bike rides.
