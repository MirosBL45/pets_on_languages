pets - medicine for the soul

the dog

Nothing can compare to a long walk with your four-legged friend on a fresh, spring morning. Or watch the joy on his face when you pick up the ball and he knows it's time to play in the nearby park! Even resting at home is better in company.

Dog owners are more active

Numerous facts tell us that a dog is an great pet. To begin with, the dog will not allow you to sleep long and be lazy in the morning. With the dog, you will be forced not only to get up earlier, but also to move and take him outside. Thus, your morning will pass in a pleasant walk with your pet, which is certainly a healthier and more beautiful way to start the day.

Children are taught responsibility

Children who are obliged to take the dog for a walk or feed it regularly learn responsibility earlier, which will definitely be useful to them later in life. Also, research has shown that children who have a dog as a pet have a stronger immune system and are less likely to miss school due to illness, as well as having a higher degree of self-confidence.

Positive impact on mental health

Dog owners smile more, are happier and more relaxed, suffer less from depression and feel less lonely. Dogs undoubtedly have a beneficial effect on mental health and emotional state, because in a dog we have a friend who always listens to us, without ever judging us. A dog is a reliable and faithful friend and can be a great comfort in difficult days. Dogs simply make us happy and make our life more beautiful and fulfilling. It is up to us to protect them as much as they protect us.
